Introducing an innovative AI tool that transforms hand-drawn sketches into professional UX designs effortlessly. Built with Angular and powered by Google's Gemini AI model, this tool allows users to upload a handwritten image of their wireframe or UI concept, and within seconds, it generates a pixel-perfect, fully responsive UX design. Whether you're a designer, developer, or product manager, this tool accelerates the design process, turning raw ideas into interactive designs that can be further customized and integrated into your project.
